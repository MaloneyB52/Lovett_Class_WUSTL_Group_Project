Methods

Crime counts in 2017 were analyzed at the county level to determine its correlation with societal factors such as: the number of law enforcement employees; population density; household income; education; unemployment rate and poverty.

The Lasso regressor was found to produce better accuracy than Random Forest and Gradient Boosting. The fitting coefficients of the Lasso regressor are listed below to illustrate which factors correlate with the crime rates.
